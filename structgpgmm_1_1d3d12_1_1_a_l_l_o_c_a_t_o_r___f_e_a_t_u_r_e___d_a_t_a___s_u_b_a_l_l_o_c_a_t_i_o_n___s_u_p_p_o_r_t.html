bool gpgmm::d3d12::ALLOCATOR_FEATURE_DATA_SUBALLOCATION_SUPPORT::IsResourceAccessAlwaysCoherent

Describes multi-queue resource access behavior.

For example, if two allocations belong to the same resource where each allocation is referenced with a different command-queue, will accessing one stomp over the other. D3D12 does not guarentee such behavior is safe but it is defined per GPU vendor.
